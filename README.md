Welcome to Meowdles! 

A game made with Godot where you're a cat who owns a ramen shop. Complete orders purely based on your memory and purchase items for your shop!

Meowdles was made for MilkyWay Hack Club. 
I love playing cooking games, so I thought I would make my own. I was inspired by Ice Cream Truck - Yo.Doggies. I made most assets. Meowdles was only the 2nd game I've ever made in Godot. Learning to navigate Godot was a bit of a challenge, but through this project, I gained a lot of knowledge. Meowdles was extremely different from my 1st game. But I learned to utilize Area2D, using signals, and Singletons. The hardest part was ensuring that each scene interacted with the others.

I'm extremely proud of how Meowdles turned out!
